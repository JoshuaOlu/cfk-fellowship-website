# CFK Fellowship Brand Guide

Version: 1.0 (Draft)

---

# Brand Promise

The CFK Fellowship is a community where people grow by building educational opportunities that matter.

We believe that meaningful work develops meaningful people.

---

# Mission

To develop people who use their knowledge, skills, and creativity to expand educational opportunity.

---

# Personality

The Fellowship is:

- Warm
- Welcoming
- Thoughtful
- Purposeful
- Optimistic
- Credible
- Collaborative
- Curious

We are never arrogant, exclusive, or self-important.

---

# Our Voice

We write like a thoughtful mentor.

Professional enough to inspire confidence.

Friendly enough to invite participation.

Our writing should feel human.

---

# We Speak To

People who:

- enjoy learning
- want to contribute
- care about educational equity
- are willing to grow through service
- value collaboration

We speak to potential, not prestige.

---

# What We Call People

Preferred terms:

- Fellow
- Applicant
- Mentor
- Volunteer
- Community
- Team
- Builder
- Contributor

Avoid:

- Candidate
- Recruit
- Resource
- Human capital

People are never treated as assets.

---

# How We Talk About Growth

Growth is something people experience by doing meaningful work.

We avoid promising transformation.

Instead, we invite people into experiences that naturally develop them.

Examples:

✓ Learn by building.

✓ Grow through contribution.

✓ Develop practical skills while creating real impact.

---

# How We Talk About Service

Service is collaboration.

Not sacrifice.

Not charity.

Not obligation.

We build alongside communities rather than for them.

---

# Tone

Warm.

Hopeful.

Clear.

Confident.

Never exaggerated.

Never salesy.

Never manipulative.

---

# Headlines

Headlines should be:

Short.

Positive.

Action-oriented.

Examples:

Build educational opportunities.

Grow with purpose.

Learn by contributing.

Ideas into impact.

Knowledge in action.

---

# Body Copy

Short paragraphs.

Plain English.

Specific over abstract.

Prefer verbs over adjectives.

Example:

Instead of:

"We provide transformational leadership experiences."

Prefer:

"Work with a team to build projects that improve educational opportunity."

---

# Calls to Action

Preferred:

Apply

Join the Fellowship

Learn More

Read the Handbook

Meet the Fellows

Avoid:

Submit Now

Act Fast

Limited Time

Don't Miss Out

---

# Words We Like

Build

Learn

Grow

Serve

Community

Knowledge

Opportunity

Impact

Create

Collaborate

Curiosity

Contribution

Stewardship

---

# Words We Avoid

Disrupt

Dominate

Crush

Hack

Guru

Rockstar

Elite

Exclusive

World-class

Life-changing

These words often overpromise or create unnecessary distance.

---

# Emotional Goal

Visitors should leave the website feeling:

"I think I'd belong here."

Not:

"I hope I'm good enough."

---

# Guiding Principle

Everything we publish should reflect the belief that people grow best when they work together on meaningful problems.
